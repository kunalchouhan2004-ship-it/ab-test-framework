  Player userid 6390605 (gate_30) played 49,854 rounds

  Context:
    2nd highest player  : 2,961 rounds  (16.8x lower)
    99th percentile     : 493 rounds    (101x lower)
    Mean                : 51.87 rounds  (961x lower)
    Median              : 16.0 rounds   (3,116x lower)

  Decision: REMOVED (1 row deleted)

  Reasoning:
    - 49,854 rounds in a 7-day window implies continuous 
      non-stop play — not humanly possible
    - Gap between rank 1 and rank 2 is 16.8x — not a 
      gradual tail, a single isolated anomaly
    - Almost certainly a bot or data logging error
    - Removal is cleaner than capping given it is a 
      single isolated case

  Effect of removal:
    Mean before  : 51.87  →  Mean after  : 51.32
    Median before: 16.0   →  Median after: 16.0
    Median unchanged — outlier was only distorting the mean
